  <i>❝ I didn't have the courage to move forward. That's why I don't have friends. Playing with friends is fun. But I'm sure "friend" and "friend" are definitely different. Yesterday I watched the last episode of my favorite anime "Tatakae Wonder Guy". Initially, Wonder Guy only fought alone. He continued to be defeated time and time again. But he had the courage to keep fighting and never give up. "If you don't start moving forward, your path won't open," he always said and continued to fight. Then his courage began to move other people and they became his friends. That's why even though he looks like he will lose to his enemy, he continues to believe that all his friends will come to save him. But I don't have the courage or friends that Wonder Guy has. That's why I feel so jealous of him. When I grow up, I also want to have lots of friends who can trust each other. ❞</i>
